Snider House, c. 1870 7789 Cooper Road, Montgomery, OH, United States This frame saltbox was first owned by David Snider, a son of Cornelius Snider, one of the original founders of Montgomery. Along with other townsmen, he established a grist mill which stood at the southwest corner of Main and Cooper Roads.
