Pioneer Building, c. 1818-1819 9433 Montgomery Road, Montgomery, OH, United States The building is Federal style. Abraham Slaughter was an early owner. Upon his death in 1830, the house was auctioned off for the top bid of $50.00. In 1840 it became the residence of Dr. John Naylor, a surgeon at Camp Dennison during the Civil War.
