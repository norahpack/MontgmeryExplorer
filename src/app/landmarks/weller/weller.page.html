Weller House, c. 1807 7795 Cooper Road, Montgomery, OH, United States An excellent example of early saltbox architecture, the name was derived from salt boxes that sat on iron kitchen stoves. The house was first owned by Lodowick Weller, one of the early settlers of Montgomery. The Weller House is one of the oldest buildings in Montgomery.
