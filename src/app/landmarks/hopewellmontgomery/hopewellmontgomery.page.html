Hopewell-Montgomery Presbyterian Church, c. 1829 9431 Shelly Lane, Montgomery, OH, United States This is the oldest church in Montgomery. The church served the Presbyterians for over 130 years. The stained glass windows are memorials to early church members. The anchor in the blue pane of glass is a memorial to James Ayers, a shipbuilder who lived in Montgomery.
