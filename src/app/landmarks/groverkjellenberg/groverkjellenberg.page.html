Grover-Kjellenberg House, c. 1840 10305 Montgomery Road, Montgomery, OH, United States This house was built by Adam Grover from sun-cured bricks made on the property. Mr. Grover gave the property at the corner of Montgomery and Deerfield Roads to establish the Hopewell Cemetery. This house has been a residence, a stagecoach stop, and a tavern.
