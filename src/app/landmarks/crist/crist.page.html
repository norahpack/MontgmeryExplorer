Crist House, c.1815 9854 Zig Zag Road, Montgomery, OH, United States This Federal-style house is one of several pre-1860 farmhouses remaining in Montgomery. The early part of the house was built by Abraham Crist, who came from Orange County, N.Y., in 1807-08. The property remained in the Crist family until 1869.
