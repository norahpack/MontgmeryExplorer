Wilder-Swaim House, c. 1820 7650 Cooper Road, Montgomery, OH, United States The first residents were James and Susan Wilder who arrived from Rhode Island. William Swaim, a principal of Montgomery school, purchased the property in 1917. In 1933 the property became a golf course and in 1976 a city-owned park.
