Cameron-Feinthel House, c. 1891 7893 Cooper Road, Montgomery, OH, United States A Queen Anne style of Victorian architecture, the house has such appointments as patterned shingles and stained glass that enhance the property which first served as a Methodist Parsonage. In 1941, Edward Feinthel purchased the house. He was the village clerk in 1949. Back to historical landmarks list Audio recording performed by Frank Davis, City of Montgomery staff member.
