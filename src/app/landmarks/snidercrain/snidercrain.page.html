Snider-Crain House, c. 1818 9464 Montgomery Road, Montgomery, OH, United States This two-story frame house was built by John Snider, who was a son of Cornelius Snider, one of the founders of Montgomery. For over a century, the Snider and Crain families were owners and occupants of this landmark.
