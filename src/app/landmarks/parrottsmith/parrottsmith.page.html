Parrott-Smith House, c. 1897 7712 Cooper Road, Montgomery, OH, United States This house, situated at the beginning of a 75-foot driveway, is the best example of the Craftsman Bungalow-style house remaining in Montgomery. Important features include rough-cut stone foundation and shiplap wood siding. The house was built by brothers, Austin and Duncan Parrott.
